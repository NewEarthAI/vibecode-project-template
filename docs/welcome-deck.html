Why it's built this way

The philosophy underneath.

  • →Frame before you solve. The most expensive mistake is a thorough answer to the wrong question.
  • →Decide, don't menu. When there's a best path, take it and say so.
  • →Disagree on purpose. A devil's advocate and project-loyal reviewers keep quality honest.
  • →Verify, never assume. "Done" needs evidence — a test, a deploy, a check.
