## How to Seed

When you have a clear positioning thesis — from a conversation, a council session, or an off-the-cuff founder statement — capture it **verbatim** here before the insight decays. Don't polish. Don't formalize. Just capture.

Format:

```
## v0.1 — {{YYYY-MM-DD}} (raw capture)

### Core thesis
{{Founder's exact words describing what we are}}

### Two-mode GTM (if applicable)
{{How we go to market — platform vs. integration vs. agency vs. etc.}}

### Full-cycle scope
{{What parts of the customer journey we serve}}

### Differentiators (our moat)
{{What we win on}}

### Non-differentiators (not our battle)
{{What we cede to competitors without fighting}}

### Customer promise
{{What observable outcome we deliver}}

### Status: v0.1 — draft, awaiting refinement
```

## When Competitor Research Should Trigger Positioning Updates

A competitor profile's Differentiation Hypothesis should force a positioning review when:

- Two or more competitors occupy our claimed differentiator
- A competitor's Integration Hypothesis suggests a partnership opportunity that shifts GTM motion
- Rubric scoring shows we rank below a competitor on a dimension we claim to win
- User reviews of competitors describe a JTBD we hadn't named

Log the review trigger + outcome in [`../decisions-log.md`](../decisions-log.md).
