# the agency — Vision & Strategy

## Problem Statement

AI automation agencies need a centralized system to manage clients, projects, automations, and business operations across multiple ventures. Currently, the agency's operational data is scattered across Airtable, Supabase, Google Drive, n8n, and email — making it impossible to get a unified view of business health, client status, and project progress.

**For whom**: the agency's 2-person founding team managing an AI automation agency, a SaaS product (a SaaS app), and a property business.

## Solution

A central command center repo that:
1. Maintains awareness of all clients, projects, and automations
2. Provides unified business visibility (KPIs, revenue, costs, profitability)
3. Standardizes the client lifecycle (Lead → Delivery → Monitoring)
4. Automates 50%+ of business operations
5. Augments 20-30% of remaining operations with AI
6. Serves as the knowledge base for cross-project intelligence

## Success Metrics (3-6 months)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Business operations automated | 50%+ |
| Operations AI-augmented | 20-30% |
| Manual work remaining | Only high-ROI strategic sessions |
| Client visibility | Real-time status across all clients/projects |
| Revenue tracking | Consolidated P&L across all ventures |
| Client pipeline | Formalized Lead → Delivery → Monitor flow |
| New clients onboarded | 2-3 additional |

## Non-Goals

- Not replacing individual project repos (each keeps its own codebase)
- Not building a client-facing portal (yet)
- Not replacing dedicated CRM tools entirely (evaluating ClickUp)
- Individual client project details are managed in their own repos

## Constraints

| Constraint | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Team size | 2-person core team |
| Budget | ~$500/mo for AI tooling, cost-conscious |
| Tools | Claude Code Max ($200/mo), n8n, Supabase, Lovable, OpenAI |
| Timeline | Iterative — ship fast, improve continuously |
| Approach | Effective and minimalistic over over-engineered |

## Strategic Direction

the agency is evolving from an AI automation agency into an **AI transformation partner**:
1. **AI Maturity Audits** — Assess businesses' AI readiness (paid service)
2. **Custom Proposals** — Based on audit findings
3. **Implementation** — Build and automate with n8n, Supabase, AI
4. **Monitoring** — Ongoing optimization and reporting

### Industry Focus
- **Logistics** — Supply chain and operations automation
- **PropTech** — Real estate deal analysis, transaction coordination, investor matching
